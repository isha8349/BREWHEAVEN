Our Story

The journey from coffee enthusiasts to coffee makers

The Beginning

BrewHaven was founded in 2021 in Indore by three college friends and coffee lovers: Aanya Mehta, Vihaan Rao, and Kabir Shah. What started as late-night conversations over cups of coffee in their college canteen evolved into a shared dream of creating a space where coffee wasn't just a beverage, but an experience.

After graduation, instead of following conventional career paths, the trio embarked on a journey across India and parts of Southeast Asia, exploring coffee cultures, techniques, and traditions. They returned with a vision: to create a café that would merge global coffee cultures with a uniquely Indian twist.

With personal savings, family support, and a small business loan, BrewHaven opened its doors in Saket Nagar, quickly becoming a favorite among locals for its innovative coffee blends and warm atmosphere.

Our Sustainability Commitment

At BrewHaven, sustainability isn't just a buzzword—it's woven into everything we do:

  • Responsible Sourcing: We work directly with small-scale coffee farmers who practice sustainable agriculture.
  • Waste Reduction: Our packaging is compostable, and we offer discounts to customers who bring reusable cups.
  • Energy Efficiency: Our café uses energy-efficient appliances and LED lighting throughout.
  • Water Conservation: We've implemented water-saving techniques in our brewing processes and washrooms.
  • Community Gardens: We maintain a small herb garden for our culinary needs and as an educational tool for local schools.
