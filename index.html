Our Story

Founded in 2021 by three college friends and coffee lovers: Aanya Mehta, Vihaan Rao, and Kabir Shah, BrewHaven was born from a shared passion for exceptional coffee and community spaces.

We've created a cozy yet modern café that merges global coffee cultures with a uniquely Indian twist. Our mission is simple: Brew Bold. Live Warm.
